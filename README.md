# Credit-Card-Consumption-Prediction
To predict the average credit card expenditure by the customers for next three months.


<h3>Problem Statement</h3>

<ul><li>Based on the consumption & spending patterns and spends of a customer, the banks create a strategic partnership with vendors for discounts and other plans to reward/retain customers and also tailor loans or financial products.</li><li>
To understand these patterns thoroughly and get insights on the customer persona & the spending patterns, CMB Bank has given customer details, like age, gender, and other demographics.</li><li>
To predict the average spend for a set of customers for the next 3 months, based on the data given by bank.</li></ul>
